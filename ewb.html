What we are?

EWB-OUTR is a student chapter of the international organization Engineers without Borders (EWB).

We seek to promote collaboration so that collectively we can achieve more than the sum of our parts and offer sustainable solutions in the areas of Water, Sanitation, Energy, Health & Education to improve the quality of life for common people. Learn more

Our vision

Every Engineering student has the opportunity to achieve his/her fullest potential, undertake real-life sustainable social projects and participate in and contribute to all aspects of life

Our goals

All projects and work of the EWB-CET Student Chapter must reflect the need and principle of protection of the planet’s ecosystems, locally and globally
